Accreditation

Accreditation confirms that an organisation has met the standards set out in the SPSC Code of Conduct.

Benefits of Accreditation

  • Recognition as an approved organisation under the SPSC framework
  • Inclusion on the public register
  • Ability to display the SPSC accreditation badge
  • Access to guidance and regulatory support
  • Increased public trust and credibility

Requirements

  • Company registration details
  • Director or management information
  • Safeguarding policies
  • Public liability insurance
  • Evidence of training procedures
  • Transparent pay structures
  • Complaints handling procedures
