Report a Concern

The Street Periodical Standards Council takes all complaints seriously. If you have experienced or witnessed behaviour that may breach the SPSC Code of Conduct, you can submit a report using the form below.

Examples of issues you can report

  • Misleading or aggressive sales tactics
  • Disrespectful behaviour towards members of the public
  • Concerns about worker treatment or safeguarding
  • Misrepresentation of an organisation or publication
  • Breaches of professional conduct

What happens next

  1. The complaint is reviewed by SPSC.
  2. The relevant organisation may be contacted for clarification.
  3. An investigation may be conducted.
  4. Appropriate action will be taken where necessary.
