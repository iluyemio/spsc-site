SPSC Code of Conduct

The Code of Conduct sets out the professional and ethical standards expected of organisations operating in the street periodical sector.

What the Code Covers

  • Professional conduct when engaging with the public
  • Safeguarding of young workers and vulnerable individuals
  • Transparency around pay and working conditions
  • Health and safety during street-based work
  • Respect for public spaces and communities
  • Fair and transparent complaints procedures

Professional Conduct

Representatives must be courteous, honest, and avoid misleading or aggressive sales tactics.

Safeguarding & Welfare

Organisations must supervise workers appropriately, particularly young people and vulnerable adults.
