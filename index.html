About SPSC

The Street Periodical Standards Council is the independent regulatory body overseeing organisations that sell magazines and other periodicals in public spaces.

Our role is to bring the sector under one professional framework, regulate practices, investigate complaints, and ensure accredited organisations uphold the SPSC Code of Conduct.
