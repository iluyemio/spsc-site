About the Street Periodical Standards Council

The Street Periodical Standards Council (SPSC) is the independent standards body for organisations selling magazines and periodicals in public spaces across the United Kingdom.

The council was established to address inconsistent practices within the sector and to create a professional and accountable framework that benefits both workers and the public.

Our Mission

  • Protect the public from misleading or inappropriate practices
  • Promote ethical employment opportunities for young people
  • Ensure organisations operate transparently and responsibly
  • Sustain and enhance public confidence in the sector
  • Establish consistent standards across the United Kingdom

Our Role

SPSC sets standards through the Code of Conduct, accredits organisations that meet those standards, investigates complaints, monitors compliance, and promotes best practice.

Standards

We maintain the professional framework for organisations operating in the street periodical sector.

Public Protection

We work to protect the public and young workers from unsafe, misleading, or exploitative practices.

Accountability

We provide a route for reporting concerns and taking proportionate action where standards are breached.
